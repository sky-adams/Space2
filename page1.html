Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system and it is the closest planet in our solar system to the sun. Mercury does a full loop around the sun every 88 days and Mercury is named after Mercury the Roman Deity the messenger to the gods. Also Mercury like Venus orbits The Sun in Earth's orbit and can be seen from the Earth in the early morning or in the evening.
