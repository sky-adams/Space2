Uranus

Uranus, the only planet named after a Greek god, Ouranos, the go . It has the third largest planetary radius and the fourth largest planetary mass. Its radius spans across 15,759 miles. Uranus is the 7th planet from the sun and it is classified as an Ice giant. Uranus is consisted of hydrogen and helium but it's classified as an ice giant because it's mostly made up of water, ammonia, and methane. The methane gives Uranus its blue tint. It also has the coldest atmosphere. Uranus is on a tilted axis, it also has 27 moons. 1 earth day on Uranus is 17 hours and 14 minutes, making 1 year equal to 84 earth years.
