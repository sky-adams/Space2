Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system and it is the 5th planet away from the sun.
Jupiter is 27 km tall and 43,441 miles wide.
Named after the roman god, Jove, king of all gods, Jupiter is called "King of Planets."
Classified as a gas giant, Jupiter is mostly consisted of hydrogen and helium. Around Jupiter is a planetary ring system that is made of ammonia and water. This planet has 69 moons and is the fourth brightest thing in the night sky.
Having the shortest day in the solar system, their day is 10 Earth hours and 1 year is 4,333 days.
